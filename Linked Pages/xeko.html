Introduction

Xeko is the one of the main characters in the Doraemon comic series, written by Fujiko F Fujio. He was the son of a wealthy family and dreamed of becoming a fashion designer. He was very quick with his tongue and frequently went on with Chaien to bully Nobita. However, he was also a very dedicated friend when the group is in dire need.

Noted skills

  • Mechanical gears control: As Xeko played with toy ships and cars since he was small, he was very skilled with using console to control objects He can easily learnt to effectively controls Doremon's gears in races and other difficult situations. Moreover, his tongue also helped the group get out of multiple sensitive situations in their adventures.
  • Short stature: Xeko was the shortest in the group, and he is very sensitive about jokes on his stature.

Possible Ending

The author never finished the series. As a result, a group of fan has theorized an ending which was widely accepted:

As Doremon ran out of battery, Nobita apparently hid his friends the truth and simply told them Doremon has departed for the future. In the future, Xeko became a successful businessman (apparently taking over the family businesses). When Nobita was interviewed after his break-through technology, Dekhi, the president and another of their childhood friend, revealed that Doremon was still alive. Dekhi has also prevented every interference from the future, so that Nobita would be determine to become a scientist and create the technology that the world need.
